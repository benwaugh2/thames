The Authors

Born in New Zealand, Denis Waugh has been one of the foremost British landscape photographers for the past twenty years, his work appearing in books, magazines and newspapers throughout the world. Searching the Thames comprises photographs taken over the last five years for his personal portfolio. Limited edition prints from this project are now available for sale. Each one is signed and numbered and offered with a certificate of authenticity. Contact Denis Waugh for further details and prices at waugh@waitrose.com.

Priscilla Waugh, also a New Zealander, is a distinguished and award-winning travel writer whose work has appeared in the Independent, Telegraph and Guardian newspapers and in magazines both in Britain and abroad. She is a fervent campaigner for the Arts and the Environment and her writing has been translated into several European languages and used as a teaching aid for foreign students of English. She is happy to undertake a variety of commissions, including ghost-writing autobiographies. Contact Priscilla Waugh at priscilla.waugh@ntlworld.com.
